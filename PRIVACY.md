# Privacy Policy — Interval Timer

**Last updated: April 14, 2026**

Interval Timer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device with no network connections, no analytics, no tracking, and no accounts.

No data is shared with third parties.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, open an issue at https://github.com/twitzelbos/interval_timer/issues.
